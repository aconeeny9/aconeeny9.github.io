About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at Northwestern University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science through the McCormick School of Engineering. I am also pursuing a concentration in Legal Studies.

On campus I am involved in Spoon University, which is Northwestern's only print and digital food publication, the Society of Women Engineers (SWE), and Women in Computing (WIC).

When I am not completing classwork, I love to spend time with my friends, go hiking, or talk about whatever new book I am reading. I also love baking and have perfected my recipe for maple cinnamon scones.
